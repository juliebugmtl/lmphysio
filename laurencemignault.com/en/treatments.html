Treatment Tools

The techniques used in order to achieve treatment goals:

  • Orthopedic manual therapy: mobilizations used to decrease pain, increase range of motion, facilitate movement and reduce inflammation of affected tissues and joints.
  • Neuromuscular and myofascial release
  • Postular analysis and rehabilitation
  • Rehabilitation through exercises
  • Motor control
  • Proprioception
  • Electrotherapy (TENS, interferential current, etc.)
  • Thermotherapy (hot, cold)
  • Taping (McConnell, Neuro-proprioceptive)
  • Correction and education: advice and exercises to improve postural hygiene (sitting, standing, sports), lifestyle, etc.
